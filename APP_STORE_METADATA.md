## Full Description (Google Play)
Thryftverse is the social-first second-hand marketplace built for modern resale.

Discover unique listings, chat with buyers and sellers, and move faster from upload to checkout with a sleek mobile experience.

What you can do on Thryftverse:
- Shop curated second-hand fashion and lifestyle items
- List your items in minutes with rich media and clean pricing
- Chat directly with buyers and sellers
- Save favorites, track activity, and manage your profile
- Join Trade Hub experiences including timed auctions and co-ownership pools

Trade Hub highlights:
- Auctions: Discover and launch time-boxed auctions, place bids, and follow active markets
- Co-Own: Participate in shared ownership pools with transparent unit pricing and performance signals

Why users choose Thryftverse:
- Fast, mobile-first UX
- Community marketplace feel
- Transparent pricing and activity context
- Built-in features for both casual sellers and power traders

Safety and trust:
- Account and transaction safeguards
- Clear market activity surfaces
- Ongoing improvements for compliance and platform integrity

Download Thryftverse and join the next generation of second-hand commerce.
